MediTwin

Phase 1 (Foundation)

Goal

Creates a real-time digital twin of every human body for preventive medicine.

Plan

Map organ-level models and simulate therapy responses to act as the central source of truth for the patient.

Deep Dive

MediTwin represents the core intelligence engine. It creates a dynamic digital replica of an individual's biology, allowing all other subsidiary AIs to run simulations, forecast aging trajectories, and model therapeutic interventions before any physical action is taken.
